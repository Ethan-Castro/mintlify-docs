## v1.0 — 2026-04-22 — EC

**Initial publish.** Converted the Mintlify starter kit into the CSI HPCC documentation and educational site.

Added:

- Home, [Overview](/overview), [Accounts & access](/accounts-access), [Quickstart](/quickstart).
- [Systems & hardware](/systems), [Software & modules](/software-modules), [Storage & quotas](/storage).
- [Job submission](/job-submission) with SLURM templates for serial, OpenMP, MPI, hybrid, GPU, and job arrays.
- [Policies](/policies), [Support](/support), [Training](/training), [FAQs](/faqs).
- Mermaid diagrams for system architecture and the support escalation path.
- Navigation tabs for **Documentation** and **Support**, plus quick anchors to the HPCC portal, wiki, and ticket system.

Known gaps (tracked with `<Note>` callouts in the relevant pages) to verify against authoritative CSI/CUNY sources before wider publication:

- Exact per-node Arrow hardware specs (CPU model, core count, RAM, GPU model/memory).
- Current service status of Penzias, Appel, and Salk.
- Password expiration cadence and account renewal windows.
- Staff phone numbers, office locations, and helpdesk appointment hours.
